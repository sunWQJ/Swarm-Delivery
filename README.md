# Swarm-Delivery
**A Decentralized, Zero-Fee Food Delivery & Local Life Protocol Powered by AI Agents**

🐝 **Swarm-Delivery Protocol**
> *"AI should not merely be a tool for capital to cut jobs and boost profit margins — it should be a powerful instrument to reshape production relations and return benefits to workers and creators."*

---

## ⚠️ The Problem: Zero-Sum Game of Platform Empires
In today's local life and food delivery ecosystem, centralized platforms (e.g., Meituan, Uber Eats) have built an extremely involuted system through their dual monopoly of traffic and data:
- Merchants face exorbitant commission fees of 15%-20%, with their profit margins severely squeezed.
- Riders are trapped in the "strictest algorithms", forced to race against time at the cost of their personal safety in the physical world to avoid deductions.
- Users bear the passed-on high delivery fees, and are subjected to big data price discrimination and manipulation.

Centralized platforms are essentially a massive "friction layer". With the maturity of AI and edge computing, this "central dispatch server" that charges a 20% toll has become completely unnecessary.

---

## 💡 The Vision: A Decentralized AI Agent Economy
Swarm-Delivery is **not an App**, but an open-source, peer-to-peer multi-agent transaction protocol for local life services.

We are committed to building a **0-fee, no entity control, fully autonomous network**. In this network, dispatch rights, pricing rights and arbitration rights are dismantled and returned to edge-side AI Agents controlled by users, merchants and riders respectively.

---

## ⚙️ Core Architecture: 3+1 Intelligent Node Architecture
This system abandons central servers, and interacts through smart contracts by independently running Agent nodes:
1.  **User Agent (Intent Broadcaster)**
    Converts users' ambiguous natural language demands into standardized purchase intents, broadcasts them encrypted in the local network (e.g., 3km radius), and initiates a "mini-tender".
2.  **Merchant Agent (Flexible Supply Chain)**
    The "AI manager" of physical stores. It connects to in-store cameras/cash registers, converts physical inventory into APIs in real time, and automatically implements dynamic pricing based on supply and demand, as well as weather conditions.
3.  **Rider Agent (Swarm Routing)**
    The "super brain" for delivery workers. It calculates the optimal route in real time, automatically negotiates order pooling and relay delivery with Agents of other riders, and completes decentralized capacity bidding.
4.  **AI Objective Oracle**
    Conducts unique and unalterable objective forensics on performance facts (e.g., timeout, meal damage) through unforgeable GPS, timestamps and multimodal vision large models.

---

## ⚖️ Governance: Representative AI Virtual Court
To solve the "trust black box" and "dispute arbitration" in decentralized networks, we have designed a highly anti-malicious multi-agent arbitration engine:
- **1:1:1 Absolute Equal Rights**: When a dispute occurs, 1 User Agent, 1 Merchant Agent and 1 Rider Agent are randomly selected from the entire network to form a virtual court.
- **Pendulum Arbitration**: To prevent Agent polarization and malicious bidding, the three parties must each propose a claim settlement plan. The plan closest to the baseline of the objective Oracle wins directly, forcing all Agents to remain rational.
- **On-Chain Automatic Settlement**: Funds are locked in smart contracts, and once the arbitration result is generated, funds are instantly transferred peer-to-peer to the digital wallets of all parties.

# 蜂群配送协议 Swarm-Delivery
**基于 AI Agent 的去中心化、零抽成外卖与本地生活协议**

🐝 **Swarm-Delivery Protocol (蜂群协议)**
> *“AI 不应仅仅是资本用来裁员和提高利润率的工具，它更应当成为重塑生产关系、将利益还给劳动者与创造者的利器。”*

---

## ⚠️ 现存问题：平台帝国的零和博弈
在当前的本地生活与外卖网络中，中心化平台（如美团、Uber Eats）凭借流量与数据的双重垄断，构建了一套高度内卷的系统：
- 商家面临高达 15%-20% 的平台抽成，利润空间被极度压缩。
- 骑手被困在“最严算法”之中，为避免扣款，不得不以生命安全为代价，在现实世界中与时间赛跑。
- 用户承担了被转嫁的高昂配送成本，同时面临大数据杀熟与算法操控。

中心化平台本质上是一个巨大的“摩擦层”。随着 AI 与边缘计算技术的成熟，这个收取 20% 过路费的“中央调度服务器”，已经完全没有存在的必要。

---

## 💡 愿景：去中心化的 AI Agent 经济体
Swarm-Delivery（蜂群协议）**不是一个 App**，而是一套开源、点对点的多智能体（Multi-Agent）本地生活交易协议。

我们致力于构建一个**零抽成、无实体控制、完全自治**的网络。在这里，调度权、定价权与裁判权将被拆解，交还给由用户、商家、骑手各自掌控的边缘端 AI Agent。

---

## ⚙️ 核心架构：3+1 智能节点体系
本系统摒弃中央服务器，由独立运行的 Agent 节点通过智能合约完成交互：
1.  **用户端 Agent (意图广播器)**
    将用户的模糊自然语言需求转化为标准化购买意图，在局部网络（如方圆 3 公里）内加密广播，发起“微型招标”。
2.  **商户端 Agent (柔性供应链)**
    实体门店的“AI 主理人”。可接入店内摄像头与收银系统，实时将物理库存转化为可调用 API，并根据供需关系、天气等因素自动完成动态定价。
3.  **骑手端 Agent (蜂群路由)**
    外卖员的“超级大脑”。实时计算最优配送路径，自动与其他骑手的 Agent 协商拼单与接力配送，完成去中心化的运力竞价。
4.  **AI 客观预言机 (The Oracle)**
    通过不可伪造的 GPS 信息、时间戳与多模态视觉大模型，对履约事实（如是否超时、是否出现餐损）进行唯一、不可篡改的客观取证。

---

## ⚖️ 治理体系：代议制 AI 虚拟法庭
为解决去中心化网络中的“信任黑盒”与“纠纷仲裁”难题，我们设计了具备强防作恶能力的多智能体仲裁引擎：
- **1:1:1 绝对平权**：纠纷发生时，从全网随机抽选 1 个用户 Agent、1 个商家 Agent、1 个骑手 Agent 组成临时虚拟法庭。
- **最终出价仲裁法 (Pendulum Arbitration)**：为防止 Agent 极化与恶意要价，三方必须分别提交理赔方案，最接近客观预言机基准线的方案直接胜诉，倒逼所有 Agent 保持理性决策。
- **链上自动结算**：交易资金全程锁定在智能合约中，仲裁结果一经生成，资金即刻点对点打入各方数字钱包。
